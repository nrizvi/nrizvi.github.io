I am a multi-award winning CSE PhD Candidate at UC San Diego. I work on fine-tuning and evaluating LLMs, data collection and quality assurance for LLM benchmarking, and prompt engineering for LLM safety and alignment. I have first-author publications at FAccT, CHI, and CSCW, as well as ACL (in submission, with average 4/5 score), and served as an invited speaker at top conferences including NeurIPS, SigCSE, GHC, and Tapia. I even co created the curriculum for the human-centered AI course at UCSD. My research has been featured in both national and international media, reaching 21 nations, and even earned me a commendation letter from former VP Kamala Harris!
