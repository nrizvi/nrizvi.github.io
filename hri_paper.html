Autism is multi-dimensional and can be understood in a variety of ways! Yet...

  • * 90.8% of HRI studies focus on providing diagnosis or treatment for autism
  • * Only 8.45% of papers included a representative sample of women and girls
  • * Only 6.6% of studies included autistic adults
  • * 89.4% of studies did not even assess whether autistic people need the robots we are designing and developing for them!
